Baked Chicken

Description

Baked chicken is a simplistic yet elegant dish.

You will be surprised how good this recipe is with the addition of some basic spices

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  1. Sprinkle salt and pour lemon juice on chicken
  2. Place chicken in the over at 350 degrees.
  3. Bake for 15 minutes.
  4. Enjoy!
